What is TSA?

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national, student-led organization that empowers students to grow as leaders in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

With over 250,000 members nationwide, TSA fosters problem-solving, collaboration, innovation, and critical thinking through over 70 competitive events, leadership opportunities, and career-oriented activities.

About Our Chapter

Peachtree Ridge TSA (PRHS TSA) is one of the largest and most decorated chapters in the region. We participate in local, state, and national competitions, often bringing home top awards in video production, systems control, biotechnology, and more.

Beyond competitions, our chapter emphasizes leadership development, service, and collaboration. Our members become confident communicators, creative thinkers, and capable engineers—prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.
